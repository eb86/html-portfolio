EB's Favorite Movies

Elaine's Top 3 movie picks.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

This movie is what made me more interested in reading. I saw this movie when I was young so it reminds me of the only times in my younger years when I could escape my toxic world.

Pan's Labyrinth

Pan's Labyrinth is probably one of the best movies made about lores. It has fantasy element. It has horror element. It has thriller element. It has social and family element. I can't wait until my daughters are old enough to watch this.

Legally Blonde

Who doesn't love Elle Woods? She's so inspiring! She broke the stereotype of girly-girls being bimbos. She made it known to everyone that you can be pretty, and reasonably vain, and into fashion, while taking on a whole room full of intelligent (sometimes arrogant and judgmental) people, and prove that you belong there. All in high heels!
